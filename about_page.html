THE BRAND

The King Taste Restaurant has developed superb takeout and dine-in
servies in and around Kiribathgoda. King Taste was established in 2017

in the way that we can furnish our new and faithful customers with
extraordinary-tasting Sri Lanakan dishes. We offer the best local cullinary
experience and we humbly welcome you to visit us and have your taste buds astonished.
